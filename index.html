BLOOD ON THE CLOCKTOWER

Blood on the Clocktower is a social deduction game for 5–20 players. Each player is secretly assigned a character. The good team (Townsfolk & Outsiders) tries to find and execute the Demon before everyone is dead. The evil team (Minions & Demon) tries to survive, bluff, and mislead. Each night, the Demon kills a player; each day, the town debates and may execute one player by vote. Characters have unique abilities that provide information, protection, or chaos. Even dead players get one final vote — nobody is eliminated from the conversation.
